Honey Cup

This is a setup that exists. Guide source: Yuu's wiki.
Honey Cup is yet another TD 8L PC opener thing. Notably, it has some nice low kpp to build because you can DAS preserve I_tetraminoL_tetraminoS_tetramino. The main path results in a field with 96.87% 8L PC chance.



Honey Cup is buildable when I_tetramino>L_tetramino>S_tetramino. The J_tetramino has 2 choice placements before the next bag.
Form A has higher coverage and its bag2 has 85% coverage to go into the preferred Honey Cup field (96.87% 8L PC chance), with a compromise build with 70.63% 8L PC chance.
Form B has a bag2 with 95% coverage to go into the preferred Honey Cup field, but its compromise build has 63.61% with cursed solutions.
